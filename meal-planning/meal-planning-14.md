# Section 14: Sample Meal Plans & Shopping Lists

[← Back to Main Meal Planning Guide](meal-planning.md) | [← Previous: Nutrition Considerations in Meal Planning](meal-planning-13.md)

---

Ready-to-use meal plans with complete shopping lists. Choose the plan that fits your needs, or use as inspiration for creating your own!

## How to Use These Plans

**Each Plan Includes:**
- 5-7 days of meals
- Breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks
- Complete shopping list organized by category
- Prep instructions
- Nutritional highlights

**Adapt to Your Needs:**
- Swap proteins (chicken for turkey, etc.)
- Substitute vegetables based on preference
- Adjust portions for household size
- Repeat favorite meals

## Week 1: Beginner-Friendly Plan

### Overview
- **Focus:** Simple recipes, common ingredients, easy techniques
- **Prep Time:** 2-3 hours
- **Difficulty:** Easy
- **Perfect for:** New to meal planning, basic cooking skills

### The Meals

#### Breakfast (All Week)
**Overnight Oats (5 servings)**
- 1/2 cup oats
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1/2 cup berries
- 1 tbsp honey

**Prep:** Mix in mason jars Sunday night, refrigerate

#### Lunch (Monday-Friday)

**Option 1: Chicken and Rice Bowls (3 servings)**
- 5 oz grilled chicken breast
- 1 cup brown rice
- 1 cup steamed broccoli
- Teriyaki sauce

**Option 2: Turkey Sandwiches (2 servings)**
- Whole wheat bread
- Sliced turkey
- Lettuce, tomato, cheese
- Mustard/mayo
- Apple on side

#### Dinner (Monday-Friday)

**Monday: Spaghetti with Meat Sauce**
- Whole wheat pasta
- Ground beef
- Jarred marinara sauce
- Side salad

**Tuesday: Sheet Pan Chicken and Vegetables**
- Chicken thighs
- Potatoes
- Carrots
- Olive oil, seasonings

**Wednesday: Tacos**
- Ground beef
- Taco shells
- Lettuce, tomato, cheese, salsa
- Rice and beans

**Thursday: Stir-Fry**
- Chicken breast
- Frozen stir-fry vegetables
- Rice
- Bottled stir-fry sauce

**Friday: Breakfast for Dinner**
- Scrambled eggs
- Toast
- Bacon or sausage
- Fruit

#### Snacks (Daily)
- Greek yogurt
- String cheese
- Apples with peanut butter
- Baby carrots with hummus

### Shopping List

**PROTEINS:**
- 2 lbs chicken breasts
- 1 lb chicken thighs
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 lb ground beef (for tacos)
- 1 lb sliced turkey (deli)
- 1 dozen eggs
- Bacon or sausage (1 package)

**GRAINS:**
- Oats (1 canister)
- Brown rice (1 bag)
- Whole wheat pasta (1 box)
- Whole wheat bread (1 loaf)
- Taco shells (1 box)

**VEGETABLES:**
- Broccoli (2 heads)
- Carrots (2 lbs)
- Potatoes (2 lbs)
- Lettuce (2 heads)
- Tomatoes (4-5)
- Baby carrots (1 bag)
- Frozen stir-fry vegetables (1 bag)

**FRUITS:**
- Berries (2 containers)
- Apples (5-6)
- Bananas (bunch)

**DAIRY:**
- Milk (1/2 gallon)
- Greek yogurt (large container)
- String cheese (1 package)
- Shredded cheese (1 bag)

**PANTRY/CONDIMENTS:**
- Chia seeds (small bag)
- Honey
- Peanut butter
- Hummus (1 container)
- Marinara sauce (1 jar)
- Stir-fry sauce (1 bottle)
- Teriyaki sauce (1 bottle)
- Taco seasoning (1 packet)
- Salsa (1 jar)
- Olive oil
- Salt, pepper, garlic powder

**CANNED/DRIED:**
- Canned black beans (2 cans)

**Estimated Cost:** $100-120 for one person/week

### Prep Instructions (Sunday)

**2-Hour Prep:**

1. **Make overnight oats** (15 min)
   - Mix 5 jars
   - Refrigerate

2. **Cook brown rice** (30 min)
   - 3 cups cooked
   - Portion into containers

3. **Grill chicken breasts** (20 min)
   - Season with salt, pepper, garlic
   - Grill or bake at 400°F for 18-20 min
   - Cool and portion for lunches

4. **Steam broccoli** (10 min)
   - Steam until tender-crisp
   - Portion for lunches

5. **Assemble lunch containers** (15 min)
   - 3 chicken bowls (chicken, rice, broccoli, teriyaki in small cup)

6. **Prep sandwich ingredients** (10 min)
   - Wash and slice vegetables
   - Store in container

**Done! Dinners cook fresh each evening (30-45 min each)**

## Week 2: Plant-Based Plan

### Overview
- **Focus:** Vegetarian/vegan-friendly meals
- **Prep Time:** 2-3 hours
- **Difficulty:** Easy-Medium
- **Perfect for:** Meatless Monday enthusiasts, vegetarians, trying plant-based

### The Meals

#### Breakfast (All Week)
**Smoothie Bowls (5 servings)**
- Frozen berries
- Banana
- Almond milk
- Protein powder (plant-based)
- Toppings: Granola, chia seeds, fresh fruit

**Prep:** Pre-portion frozen ingredients in bags

#### Lunch (Monday-Friday)

**Buddha Bowls (5 servings)**
- Quinoa
- Roasted chickpeas
- Roasted sweet potato
- Kale (massaged with lemon)
- Avocado (add fresh)
- Tahini dressing

#### Dinner (Monday-Friday)

**Monday: Black Bean Burrito Bowls**
- Brown rice
- Black beans
- Corn
- Bell peppers and onions
- Salsa, guacamole, lime

**Tuesday: Lentil Curry**
- Red lentils
- Coconut milk
- Curry spices
- Vegetables (cauliflower, spinach)
- Serve over rice

**Wednesday: Veggie Stir-Fry with Tofu**
- Pressed and cubed tofu
- Mixed vegetables
- Brown rice
- Soy sauce, ginger, garlic

**Thursday: Pasta Primavera**
- Whole wheat pasta
- Roasted vegetables (zucchini, tomatoes, peppers)
- Olive oil, garlic, basil
- Nutritional yeast or parmesan

**Friday: Chickpea Tacos**
- Roasted chickpeas (taco seasoning)
- Corn tortillas
- Cabbage slaw
- Avocado, lime

#### Snacks (Daily)
- Hummus with vegetables
- Apple with almond butter
- Trail mix
- Energy balls (dates, nuts, oats)

### Shopping List

**PROTEINS:**
- Canned chickpeas (4 cans - 15 oz each)
- Canned black beans (3 cans)
- Red lentils (1 bag)
- Extra firm tofu (2 blocks)
- Plant-based protein powder (1 container)
- Almond butter (1 jar)

**GRAINS:**
- Quinoa (1 bag)
- Brown rice (1 bag)
- Whole wheat pasta (1 box)
- Corn tortillas (1 package)

**VEGETABLES:**
- Sweet potatoes (3 large)
- Kale (2 bunches)
- Bell peppers (4)
- Onions (2)
- Zucchini (2)
- Cauliflower (1 head)
- Spinach (1 bag)
- Cherry tomatoes (1 container)
- Cabbage (1 small head)
- Mixed stir-fry vegetables (fresh or frozen)
- Carrots (1 lb)
- Celery (1 bunch)

**FRUITS:**
- Avocados (4-5)
- Bananas (bunch)
- Apples (5)
- Frozen berries (2 bags)
- Limes (4-5)
- Lemons (2-3)

**DAIRY ALTERNATIVES:**
- Almond milk (1 half-gallon)
- Coconut milk (2 cans - full fat)

**PANTRY/CONDIMENTS:**
- Tahini (1 jar)
- Hummus (large container)
- Nutritional yeast (1 bag)
- Coconut oil
- Olive oil
- Soy sauce (or tamari)
- Curry powder
- Taco seasoning
- Cumin, garlic powder, onion powder
- Fresh ginger (1 knob)
- Fresh garlic (1 bulb)
- Fresh basil (1 bunch)
- Chia seeds
- Granola (1 bag)
- Dates (for energy balls)
- Mixed nuts (for trail mix)
- Salsa (1 jar)

**Estimated Cost:** $90-110 for one person/week

### Prep Instructions (Sunday)

**2.5-Hour Prep:**

1. **Cook grains** (40 min)
   - Quinoa (3 cups cooked)
   - Brown rice (4 cups cooked)
   - Portion into containers

2. **Roast vegetables** (40 min)
   - Sheet pan 1: Diced sweet potatoes (400°F, 30 min)
   - Sheet pan 2: Chickpeas with taco seasoning (400°F, 25 min)
   - Cool and portion

3. **Massage kale** (10 min)
   - Remove stems, chop
   - Massage with lemon juice and salt
   - Store in container

4. **Make tahini dressing** (5 min)
   - Tahini, lemon juice, garlic, water
   - Store in small container

5. **Assemble Buddha bowls** (20 min)
   - Layer: Quinoa, roasted sweet potato, chickpeas, kale
   - Keep avocado separate (add fresh)
   - Dressing on side

6. **Prep smoothie bags** (15 min)
   - 5 bags with frozen berries, banana chunks
   - Ready to blend

7. **Make energy balls** (20 min)
   - Blend dates, nuts, oats
   - Roll into balls
   - Store in container

**Dinners:** Some fresh cooking (20-40 min each night)

- Peanut butter (1 jar - $2.50)

- Marinara sauce (1 large jar - $2.50)

- Soy sauce (small bottle - $1.50)

**TOTAL ESTIMATED COST: $48-52**

### Prep Instructions (Sunday)

**1.5-Hour Prep:**

1. **Make lentil soup** (45 min)
   - Chop vegetables
   - Simmer lentils, vegetables, tomatoes, broth
   - Portion into containers

   - 4 cups cooked
   - Use for burritos and fried rice

3. **Hard-boil eggs** (15 min)
   - 6 eggs for quick protein snacks

**Most meals cook fresh in 15-20 minutes**

### Money-Saving Tips for This Plan

- Buy store brands (save 30-40%)
- Eggs are cheapest protein ($0.20 each)
- Beans and lentils go far (8-10 servings per bag/can)
- Rice is filling and cheap
- Peanut butter is nutritious and budget-friendly
- Frozen vegetables = no waste
- Make soup (stretches ingredients)

## Week 4: High-Protein Fitness Plan

### Overview
- **Focus:** 140-150g protein per day, muscle building/maintenance
- **Prep Time:** 3 hours
- **Difficulty:** Medium
- **Perfect for:** Athletes, gym-goers, high protein needs

#### Breakfast (All Week)

- 2 slices whole wheat toast
- 1/2 avocado
- Fruit

**Total Protein: ~35g**

**Protein Shake**

- 1 banana

**Total Protein: ~37g**

#### Lunch (Monday-Friday)

**Grilled Chicken Power Bowls (5 servings)**
- 6 oz grilled chicken breast (40g protein)
- 1 cup quinoa (8g protein)
- Roasted vegetables
- Olive oil

#### Afternoon Snack
**Greek Yogurt Parfait**
- 1 cup Greek yogurt (20g)
- 1/4 cup granola
- Berries
- 1 tbsp chia seeds

**Total Protein: ~22g**

#### Dinner (Monday-Friday)

**Monday: Steak and Sweet Potato**
- 6 oz sirloin steak (45g protein)
- Large sweet potato
- Broccoli

**Total Protein: ~48g**

**Tuesday: Salmon and Asparagus**
- 6 oz salmon (40g protein)
- Asparagus
- Brown rice

**Total Protein: ~45g**

**Wednesday: Turkey Meatballs and Pasta**
- Turkey meatballs - 6 oz (40g protein)
- Whole wheat pasta (8g protein)
- Marinara sauce
- Side salad

**Total Protein: ~50g**

**Thursday: Chicken Thighs and Vegetables**
- 6 oz chicken thighs (42g protein)

- Quinoa (8g protein)

**Total Protein: ~50g**

**Friday: Tilapia and Rice**
- 6 oz tilapia (42g protein)

**Total Protein: ~48g**

#### Evening Snack
**Cottage Cheese with Fruit**
- 1 cup cottage cheese (25g protein)

**GRAINS:**
- Quinoa (1 large bag)

**VEGETABLES:**

**FRUITS:**
- Bananas (bunch)

- Peanut butter (1 jar)

- Olive oil
- Marinara sauce (1 jar)

*(High protein foods are more expensive)*

**3-Hour Prep:**

1. **Cook grains** (40 min)
   - Quinoa (6 cups cooked)
   - Brown rice (4 cups cooked)

2. **Grill chicken breasts** (25 min)
   - Season 3 lbs chicken

5. **Assemble chicken power bowls** (20 min)
   - Chicken, quinoa, vegetables
   - 5 containers

6. **Prep breakfast scramble ingredients** (15 min)
   - Pre-measure egg whites
   - Dice vegetables (if adding)
   - Quick morning assembly

7. **Portion snacks** (10 min)
   - Greek yogurt parfaits
   - Cut fruit

**Other proteins cook fresh (10-15 min cooking time)**

- Frozen shrimp (quick cooking)
- Jarred sauces (no making from scratch)

**Strategy:**
- Use convenience items strategically
- Costs more but saves 5-10 hours
- Perfect for crazy-busy weeks
- No shame in shortcuts!

## Mixing and Matching

### Creating Your Own Plans

**Use These as Templates:**
- Swap proteins (chicken for turkey, beef for pork)
- Substitute vegetables based on preference or sale
- Adapt to dietary needs
- Scale portions for household size

**Building Blocks:**
- Choose 2-3 proteins
- Choose 2-3 grains
- Choose 4-5 vegetables
- Mix and match throughout week

**Example:**
- Proteins: Chicken, salmon, beans
- Grains: Rice, quinoa, pasta
- Vegetables: Broccoli, peppers, sweet potato, spinach, carrots
- = 30+ possible meal combinations!
